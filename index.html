San Diego

I grew up in San Diego starting from the age of 2. K-12 was spent with the Poway Unified School District. I returned to San Diego for a few years just before grad school from 2008 - 2010. During this time, I got a chance to explore the city beyond my immediate neighborhood and Del Mar beach, which is where I spent a lot of my high school years.

Los Angeles

I spent four years in LA as a UCLA student studying electrical engineering. I loved UCLA-- the school spirit, location, athletics, social activities, academics, etc. It wasn't until my 4th year when I started exploring LA beyond the Westwood bubble.

South Korea

During my third year at UCLA, I spent six months studying abroad in Seoul, South Korea.
